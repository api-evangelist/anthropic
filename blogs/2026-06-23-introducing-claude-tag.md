Anthropic launched Claude Tag, a Slack integration that lets teams tag @Claude as a team member to delegate tasks. Available in beta for Enterprise and Team customers and powered by Claude Opus 4.8, it builds context from channels, works asynchronously, and takes proactive initiative, with 65% of Anthropic's product team code now created by their internal version.
